Monosystems (1998 - 2000)

Monosystems was an interior fit-out and joinery company in Birmingham. I worked there as 'Technical Assistant' in between completing my A-levels and starting university. My responsibilities included setting up and managing the IT infrastructure and building several bespoke Visual Basic applications for project estimation and administration. I had built computers and written databases while I was at school, but this was my first experience of managing a network and dealing with users.

I stayed on to provide technical support after I started my degree in 1999. My favourite call ran something like this:
ME: What's the problem?
THEM: The sever's down!
ME: Are you sure it's plugged in?
THEM: Yes, all the cables are plugged in but the lights aren't on.
ME: Have you tried pressing the power button?
THEM: Hold on... yes, and it's still not coming on.
ME: Hmmm...
THEM: Er, we've had a leak and the server's sitting in a pool of water, do you think that might have something to do with it?
ME: We probably shouldn't rule that out.
